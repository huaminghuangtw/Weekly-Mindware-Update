# Wisdom I Pondered This Week

* Don’t live the same year 75 times and call it a life. Inject some planned spontaneity (micro & macro adventures) into your days.
* Deal with the big while it is still small.
* Enjoy the little things, for one day you may look back and realize they were the big things.
* Once is a mistake. Twice is a choice. Three times is a decision.
* If you live for people's approval, you will die from their rejection.
* The stupidity of people comes from having an answer for everything. The wisdom of the novel comes from having a question for everything.
