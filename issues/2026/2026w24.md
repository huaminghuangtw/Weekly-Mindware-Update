# Things I Learned This Week

* [**Calvin and Hobbes**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Calvin+and+Hobbes), created by [Bill Watterson](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bill+Watterson) and published from 1985 to 1995, is a seminal American comic strip that follows Calvin, an imaginative six-year-old, and his stuffed tiger Hobbes, who comes to life through Calvin’s eyes.
* [White Knighting](https://www.google.com/search?q=White+Knighting) (白騎士症候群) is a term used to describe a behavior where someone (often a man) intervenes in a situation to defend or rescue another person (often a woman), who may not need or want help. The motivation can be genuine concern, but it is sometimes driven by a desire for validation, ego, or to be seen as heroic.
* [EDA (Electronic Design Automation)](https://www.google.com/search?q=Electronic+Design+Automation) refers to specialized software tools that automate the design, simulation, verification, and manufacturing of electronic systems—especially **integrated circuits (ICs)** and **printed circuit boards (PCBs)**. EDA tools function like a combination of AutoCAD, Photoshop, QA, and project management for chip design. Leading companies in this field include Synopsis (新思科技), Cadence Design Systems, and Siemens EDA (Mentor Graphics). Without EDA, the development of modern electronics would not be feasible.
* [Ditto](https://www.google.com/search?q=Ditto) is an English term that means “the same as stated above” or “likewise.” It is often used to avoid repeating something that has already been mentioned. For example, if someone says, “I enjoyed the movie,” and you feel the same way, you might simply reply, “Ditto.” The word is commonly used in informal conversation and writing to express agreement or similarity.
* 「五項全能（Pentathlon）」是一種綜合性的田徑比賽，各項目成績會根據得分表換算成積分，總積分最高者獲勝。在國際賽事中較少見，多見於青年組、中學比賽或特殊的分齡賽。它包含三個田賽（跳遠、跳高、鉛球）與兩個徑賽（100m/110m 跨欄、200m 短距離跑、800m/1500m 中距離跑）項目，通常在一天內集中完成。【比較】：「現代五項（Modern Pentathlon）」：包含射擊、擊劍、游泳、馬術和跑步，非純田徑項目。
